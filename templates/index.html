How DeepDetect Works

DeepDetect uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on real and manipulated facial datasets. The model learns patterns that reveal digital manipulation, helping detect potential deepfake images.

⚠️ This project is a research prototype built for educational purposes. Detection accuracy may vary depending on image quality and manipulation methods.
